# ch06-02 英文简历动词库

> 每条 bullet 以动词过去式开头。选对动词 = 第一印象加分。

---

## 按功能分类

### 领导与管理
Led, Directed, Orchestrated, Spearheaded, Oversaw, Supervised, Managed

### 创建与设计
Designed, Architected, Developed, Established, Created, Built, Launched

### 优化与改进
Optimized, Streamlined, Automated, Restructured, Revamped, Enhanced

### 分析与研究
Analyzed, Evaluated, Researched, Assessed, Identified, Investigated

### 交付与执行
Delivered, Executed, Implemented, Deployed, Completed, Achieved

### 协作与沟通
Collaborated, Partnered, Negotiated, Presented, Liaised, Coordinated

---

## 弱动词 → 强动词

| 弱 | 强 |
|----|-----|
| Helped | Facilitated, Supported, Assisted in |
| Worked on | Executed, Delivered, Implemented |
| Did | Completed, Accomplished, Performed |
| Made | Created, Developed, Produced |
| Was part of | Contributed to, Participated in |
