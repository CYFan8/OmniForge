# RAG-KB-003: Composition & Camera for Image Generation

## Composition Rules
- Rule of thirds: place subject at grid intersections, horizon on third line
- Leading lines: roads, rivers, fences guide eye to subject
- Framing: archways, windows, branches frame the subject
- Symmetry: centered subject, balanced elements, formal composition
- Golden ratio: 1.618 spiral, natural focal point placement
- SD prompt: "rule of thirds, cinematic composition, balanced framing"

## Camera Lenses
- Wide angle (24mm): exaggerated perspective, large depth of field, distorted edges
- Normal (50mm): natural perspective, similar to human eye, balanced
- Portrait (85mm): compressed perspective, flattering, blurred background
- Telephoto (200mm): extreme compression, shallow DOF, isolate subject
- SD prompt: "85mm lens, portrait lens, shallow depth of field, bokeh"

## Depth of Field
- Shallow DOF: f/1.4-f/2.8, blurred background, portrait, isolate subject
- Medium DOF: f/5.6-f/8, some background visible, environmental portrait
- Deep DOF: f/11-f/16, everything sharp, landscape, architecture
- Bokeh: circular light blurs in background, aperture blade shape
- SD prompt: "shallow depth of field, bokeh background, f/1.8"

## Perspective
- One-point: all lines converge to single vanishing point, tunnel/road
- Two-point: two vanishing points, buildings at corner, dynamic
- Three-point: adds vertical vanishing, skyscraper/birds-eye view
- Atmospheric: distant objects fade, bluer, less contrast (aerial perspective)
- SD prompt: "dramatic perspective, wide angle, vanishing point"

## Color Theory
- Complementary: opposite on wheel (blue-orange), high contrast, cinematic
- Analogous: adjacent on wheel (blue-teal-green), harmonious, calm
- Triadic: three equally spaced (red-yellow-blue), vibrant, balanced
- Monochromatic: single hue variations, sophisticated, unified
- SD prompt: "complementary colors, teal and orange, color harmony"
