# RAG-KB-001: Human Anatomy for Image Generation

## Head-Body Proportions
- Adult: 7-7.5 heads tall | Heroic: 8 heads | Child: 4-5 heads
- Shoulder width = 2-2.5 head widths | Waist at 3 heads from top
- Arms reach mid-thigh | Hands reach mid-thigh | Legs = half total height

## Face Anatomy (Three Courts Five Eyes)
- Hairline to brow = brow to nose base = nose base to chin (equal thirds)
- Face width = 5 eye widths | Eyes at midline | Ears align brow to nose base
- SD prompt: "detailed face, symmetrical features, anatomically correct face"

## Muscle Groups
- Male: broader shoulders, visible trapezius, defined pectorals, V-taper
- Female: narrower shoulders, wider hips, softer deltoids, visible collarbone
- Key: deltoid-bicep-tricep arm mass | quadriceps-hamstring leg mass
- SD prompt: "well-defined musculature, anatomical proportions, fit physique"

## Hands & Feet
- Fingers: 3 joints each | Thumb: 2 joints | Palm = same length as middle finger
- Foot arch visible | Ankle bone prominent | Toes taper from big to small
- SD neg: "bad hands, fused fingers, extra fingers, missing fingers, bad anatomy"

## Dynamic Poses
- Contrapposto: weight on one leg, hip tilt, shoulder counter-tilt
- Running: opposite arm-leg forward, knee high, torso lean
- Sitting: pelvis rotation, spine curve, weight distribution
- SD prompt: "dynamic pose, contrapposto, natural stance, action pose"

## Skin & Hair
- Skin: subsurface scattering, pore texture, oil sheen on T-zone
- Hair: grows from follicles in clusters, follows gravity, layered strands
- SD prompt: "detailed skin texture, realistic hair strands, subsurface scattering"
- SD neg: "plastic skin, waxy, airbrushed, flat hair, helmet hair"

## Facial Expressions
- Smile: zygomaticus major lifts mouth, orbicularis oculi crinkles eyes
- Anger: corrugator furrows brow, orbicularis oris tightens mouth
- Surprise: frontalis raises brows, jaw drops open
- SD prompt: "natural expression, genuine smile, subtle emotion, micro-expression"
