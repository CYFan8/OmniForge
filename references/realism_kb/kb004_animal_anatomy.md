# RAG-KB-004: Animal Anatomy for Image Generation

## Canine (Wolf/Dog)
- Head: elongated snout, triangular ears erect, almond eyes, wet nose prominent
- Body: deep chest, tucked waist, sloping back, bushy tail
- Legs: digitigrade (walk on toes), dewclaw, thick paw pads
- Fur: double coat, thicker neck ruff, directional growth pattern
- SD prompt: "wolf anatomy, thick fur, muscular build, wild canine"

## Feline (Lion/Tiger/Cat)
- Head: round skull, forward-facing eyes, short muzzle, whisker pads
- Body: flexible spine, retractable claws, muscular shoulders
- Tiger: stripe pattern follows muscle contours, white underbelly
- Lion: male mane frames face, tawny coat, tail tuft
- SD prompt: "big cat anatomy, muscular feline, detailed fur pattern"

## Equine (Horse)
- Head: long face, large nostrils, mobile ears, strong jaw
- Body: deep barrel chest, sloping shoulder, defined withers
- Legs: single-toed hooves, cannon bone visible, powerful hindquarters
- Coat: short glossy hair, mane and tail long flowing
- SD prompt: "horse anatomy, muscular equine, flowing mane, glossy coat"

## Birds
- Wing: primary feathers (flight), secondary feathers (lift), covert feathers (cover)
- Raptor: hooked beak, forward eyes, talons, broad wings
- Waterfowl: webbed feet, waterproof feathers, long neck
- SD prompt: "bird anatomy, detailed feathers, wings spread, avian structure"

## Reptiles
- Scaly skin: overlapping scales, directional pattern, color variation
- Snake: no limbs, elongated body, forked tongue, heat pits
- Lizard: four legs, clawed toes, regenerating tail, lateral undulation
- SD prompt: "reptile scales, detailed skin texture, cold-blooded animal"

## Common SD Animal Fixes
- Extra legs: "four legs, quadruped" in prompt | "extra legs, six legs" in neg
- Wrong paws: "detailed paws, claws visible" | "bad paws, fused toes" in neg
- Weird eyes: "animal eyes, natural eye placement" | "human eyes" in neg
